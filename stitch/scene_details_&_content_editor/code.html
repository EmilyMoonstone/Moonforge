The Ambush at Blackwood Bridge

Combat

Forest

Level 4 Party

"As you step onto the ancient timbers of Blackwood Bridge, the forest sounds abruptly cease. The rushing water below seems unusually loud, masking the snap of twigs on the far bank. A heavy mist begins to coil around your ankles, cold and unnatural."

The bridge is approximately 40 feet long and 10 feet wide. It is slick with moss and moisture from the river below. Any character moving more than half speed must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity (Acrobatics) check or fall prone.

@Old Man Ricket warned the party about this crossing. If they remembered his advice about the "creaking plank," they might spot the trap.

The Ambush

Hiding in the dense foliage on the northern bank are three @Goblin Archer units and one @Goblin Shaman. They have total cover until they attack.

Tactic: The archers will focus fire on the character carrying the @Moonstone Lantern, recognizing it as a source of magical power. The Shaman will attempt to cast Entangle on the bridge itself to trap the party in the open.

Map: Blackwood Crossing

Treasure & Loot

If the party investigates the Shaman's pouch, they will find 45gp and a @Ring of Protection that seems to be made of twisted roots.
