The Arrival at Bogwater

The party arrived at the edge of the swamp just as the sun began to set, casting long, eerie shadows across the murky water. The air was thick with the scent of decay and damp earth. A low mist clung to the ground, obscuring the path ahead.

Here, the Marsh Hags are known to dwell. It is said they trade secrets for memories. The players should feel a sense of unease. Use the ambient soundtrack "Swamp Whispers" to enhance the mood.

Key Objectives

  • Locate the sunken ruins of the old temple.
  • Avoid alerting the lizardfolk patrols.
  • Find the Jade Idol before the eclipse.

DM Note: If the players choose to light a fire, roll for a random encounter immediately. The light attracts the Will-o'-wisps.

As they ventured deeper, the trees seemed to close in around them, their twisted branches reaching out like skeletal fingers...
